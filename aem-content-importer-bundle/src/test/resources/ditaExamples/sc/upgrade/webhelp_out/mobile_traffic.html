How Mobile Traffic Displays in Technology Reports

SiteCatalyst 15 includes mobile devices in all technology reports, but differentiates mobile technology from non-mobile technology.

For example, if you run a Browsers report (Visitor Profile > Technology > Browser) and apply a Visits from Mobile Device segment, the data displays under None in the report table. This functionality also applies to mobile reports that are broken down by Browser. In the affected reports, you might see an increase of visitors that do not accept cookies or JavaScript because visitors are using mobile devices.
