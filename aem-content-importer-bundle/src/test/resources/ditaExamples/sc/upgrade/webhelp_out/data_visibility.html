Data Visibility in Other SiteCatalyst Versions

After you migrate to SiteCatalyst 15, you can continue to view new and historical data in other versions of SiteCatalyst.

SiteCatalyst 13, 14, and 15 all report the same data, as illustrated in the following figure:

To view segments and breakdowns, you must use the SiteCatalyst 15 interface. Segments are available on data collected after your SiteCatalyst 15 migration date.
